Broodfin Website - Insurance & Financial Services Platform

Designed and launched a comprehensive responsive website for Broodfin, showcasing their full range of financial services including Health, Life, and Business insurance, along with mutual funds and NRI solutions.

Key Features Implemented:

  • Responsive design showcasing Health, Life, and Business insurance services
  • Comprehensive mutual funds and NRI solutions portfolio
  • Integrated user portals for Admin, Customer, and Employee access
  • Simplified "Get a Quote" flows for effective lead generation
  • SEO best practices implementation for improved search visibility
  • Optimized site performance for fast loading and smooth user experience
  • Analytics integration to monitor traffic, conversions, and portal engagement

Technical Implementation:

  • Multi-portal architecture with role-based access control
  • Streamlined quote request system with automated lead routing
  • Performance optimization for faster page load times
  • Mobile-first responsive design approach
  • SEO-friendly URL structure and meta optimization
  • Analytics dashboard for tracking user engagement and conversions

The website serves as Broodfin's primary digital platform, enabling customers to explore insurance options, request quotes, and access their accounts while providing comprehensive tools for administrators and employees to manage operations effectively.
